Who We Are

Sri Sharada Processores — A Trusted Name in Cashew Processing Since 2014

Founded in Vittal, Dakshina Kannada, Sri Sharada Processores started with a vision to deliver the finest cashew products to businesses across India. What began as a small processing unit has grown into a robust manufacturing operation serving 500+ clients.

We combine traditional cashew processing wisdom with modern quality assurance methods to ensure every product meets the highest standards of freshness, grading accuracy, and food safety.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Excellence

A timeline of growth, innovation, and relentless commitment to quality.

2014

Founded in Vittal

Started as a small-scale cashew processing unit with manual operations and a core team of 15 members in the heart of Dakshina Kannada.

2016

Expanded Product Range

Added W320 cashew nuts, raw cashew processing, and peanut varieties to our product portfolio — serving more diverse market needs.

2018

Modern Equipment Upgrade

Invested in semi-automatic grading machines, temperature-controlled storage, and modern packaging systems. Production capacity doubled.

2020

Achieved 200+ Client Network

Expanded our distribution network to cover major retail chains and wholesale markets across Karnataka, Kerala, and Maharashtra.

2023

Quality Certifications

Obtained FSSAI certification, GST registration, and implemented HACCP-aligned food safety protocols across our facility.

2025

500+ Clients & Growing

Today, we serve over 500 clients with annual processing capacity exceeding 1000 tonnes, looking towards national and international expansion.
